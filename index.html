No more tangled wires. Experience listening freedom like never before with the next generation true wireless Earbuds. The Dash Pro is re-newed from the inside for outstanding Bluetooth connection quality and amazing audio without interruption. And whenever you feel like leaving your smartphone or tablet at home, the on-board MP3 player of The Dash Pro offers storage for up to 1000 songs.
